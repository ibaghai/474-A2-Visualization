A2: Seattle Airbnb listing data by Seattle neighborhood

This visualization is based on Seattle AirBnB listing data from openAirBNB. This data shows the average price of AirBnB listings for each Seattle neighborhood. The price reflects the average price per neighborhood per night in three ways. These three ways are: the average overall listing price, the price for listings for private rooms, and the average price of listings for entire houses/apts.

Seattle AirBnB data

For this dataset I chose to do a barchart for each neighborhood and its price on a common scale . I did this because user's are easily able percieve the differences of the length of the bars relative to one another and the price of listings based on the position of the bar relative to one another for each neighborhood against one another . This is one of the most effective ways for individuals to percieve comparisons . I incorporated control buttons so that the user can comapre the average listing prices, the listing prices for private rooms, and the prices for entire houses/apts. This allows the user to quickly determine how expensive a neighborhood is for an AirBnB but also how quickly they can determine if that stays true for an entire house or a private room or if they want the prices for those use cases. This also allows the user to quickly determine what is the best use case for them and their AirBnB needs. If they are trying to book an entire house they can quickly jump to that data or if they want to compare average prices for a private room they can jump to that. Or if they want they can just browse the trends for pricing for overall prices by neighborhood. These buttons allow for many different use cases quickly and easily while relying on the ability to percieve the data on a common scale relative to each neighborhood by comparing position length . Also because it is on a scale it may be difficult to determine the exact average price per neighborhood which is why I added a hover over that provides the user with the exact price.

For the overall price button the user can quickly determine which neighborhoods are in what price ranges. By clicking through the average price for a private room or for entire houses/apts the user can see that there seems to be a high correlation for the listing prices for neighborhoods relative to one another in all three categories . However, Magnolia sees a massive spike in the price for listings for entire houses/apts which seems to bring up its overall average price. It is also interesting that Downtown seems to command a large premium for individual rooms per night, but that doesn't hold true for an entire apts/houses compared to other neighborhood nor correlate with its overall listing price average.
